# Privacy policy

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data related to the extension’s functionality (such as speedrun times and high scores) is stored locally on your device using your browser’s local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any external servers.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking tools, or third-party services.

Any network activity that occurs is solely part of the normal operation of the OpenFront game itself and is not controlled or modified by this extension.

By using this extension, you can be confident that your data remains private and under your control.
